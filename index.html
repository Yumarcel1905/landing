Introduction

Welcome to Capture Moment, the ultimate platform for aspiring photographers and creative minds. At Capture Moment, we believe that photography is more than just snapping pictures; it’s about capturing emotions, stories, and unforgettable moments.

Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced photographer, Capture Moment offers valuable resources tailored to enhance your knowledge and inspire your creativity. From in-depth tutorials on composition, lighting, and post-processing to tips on developing your unique style, we’re here to guide you every step of the way.

Our platform brings together people from all walks of life who share a passion for photography. Engage in discussions, participate in photo challenges, and expand your network with professionals and hobbyists alike. With bases in Rio, Shanghai, and Paris, Capture Moment is a truly global community, bridging diverse perspectives and creative styles. Join us on this journey to capture the world, one moment at a time.

Founder: Marceline Yu

Hello, I’m Marceline, the founder of Capture Moment. Photography has been my passion and my creative outlet for as long as I can remember.
